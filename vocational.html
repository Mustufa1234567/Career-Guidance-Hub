What is a Vocational Course?
→ The Vocational Course is a programme designed to provide students with practical skills and knowledge directly related to a specific occupation. Unlike traditional academic courses, vocational training focuses on developing hands-on experience and skills for a specific job or occupation. These courses can vary in duration, including vocational courses 2 years long, and can be accessed after different stages of education, such as vocational courses after 10th and vocational courses after 12th.

FAQs on Vocational Courses
1. What are the vocational courses available after 12th?
→ Vocational courses provide specialised knowledge, skills and qualifications in a particular field or industry. After completing 12th grade, students can pursue various options such as Diploma, Advanced Diploma and Certificate Programs in various sectors such as Engineering, Commerce, Science and Medical.

2. How do vocational courses help me?
→ Vocational courses provide on-the-job training, allowing students to gain practical experience in their chosen profession. By participating in these courses, students will develop specialist knowledge and skills which can be used to secure better jobs and better wages in the future.

3. Are there any prerequisites for vocational courses?
→ Yes, some vocational courses may have certain prerequisite criteria such as minimum educational requirement, age limit and/or prior work experience. Prior research into the course is recommended to ensure that there are no unexpected surprises when applying.

4. Are vocational courses expensive?
→ Generally speaking, vocational courses are not overly expensive. However, fees vary depending on the level of the course and the institution providing it. In addition to course fees, students may need to invest in equipment, materials or travel costs associated with the course.

5. How do I apply for a vocational course?
→ Applying for a vocational course is relatively straightforward. Most institutions offer online applications for their courses, but it is important to read all instructions carefully before submitting an application to ensure that all relevant documentation is provided. Additionally, applicants may need to attend an interview or complete aptitude tests if required.
